About us

Marseille Danse Academy is a multidisciplinary dance school that offers children and adults the opportunity to discover more than 20 different disciplines. Rather classical, do you like beautiful ballets and the presence that dance can offer you? Do you prefer Latin rhythms and couple dances? Or do you want to unleash a frenzied rock? No matter your age or taste, you will find your happiness at MDA! From Latin dances (Salsa, Bachata, Kizomba) to Rock/Swing dances, from the basics (Modern-Jazz, Classical) to urban dances (Hip-Hop, Dancehall, Street Jazz), not to mention other dances such as the Cabaret or Brazilian Dance, MDA offers a variety of dances to satisfy the frenzy of each of you. To learn more about Marseille dance academy, please visit https://marseille-danse-academy.fr/.
